Who this is for

This service is intended for researchers preparing competitive academic applications, especially in physics, STEM, data-intensive science, detector/R&D, machine learning, and interdisciplinary technical research.

  • Applicants preparing ERC, MSCA, SNF, or similar fellowship proposals
  • PhD students and postdocs applying for fellowships, postdoctoral positions, or faculty-track roles
  • Researchers who already have a draft and want external strategic feedback before submission
  • Applicants preparing for interviews, oral panels, chalk talks, or research presentations

Services & Pricing

I offer strategic feedback on academic applications and proposals, with a focus on scientific narrative, reviewer-facing clarity, feasibility, ambition, CV positioning, and institutional fit. The goal is not to rewrite your work, but to help you make the strongest possible version of your own application.

I am especially well positioned to support applications in physics, STEM, large-scale scientific collaborations, detector/R&D, machine learning, and interdisciplinary technical research. I can also provide structure- and narrative-focused feedback for broader academic applications.

Perspective informed by ERC Starting Grant Step 2 selection / A ranking and MSCA evaluation experience.

Typical turnaround: 5-10 working days depending on scope, document length, and urgency. Short-deadline reviews may be possible depending on availability.

Scope and pricing notes

Prices are introductory and may depend on scope, urgency, document length, and the number of review rounds. A precise scope, timeline, and deliverable list is agreed before work begins. Additional rounds, urgent reviews, or substantially longer documents may be quoted separately.

Payment is normally agreed before the review starts. For the first version of this service, payment can be arranged manually by bank transfer, Wise, or another mutually agreed method. Online payment links may be added later.

Ethics note: This is a review and consulting service, not a ghostwriting service. I do not guarantee funding, selection outcomes, interview invitations, or job offers. I do not share confidential evaluator information, and I do not advise on applications where I have a conflict of interest.

Privacy

If you contact me by email, I will process the information you send only to respond to your inquiry and, if agreed, to provide the requested consulting service. This may include academic CVs, proposal drafts, application material, reviewer comments, or related documents.

Client material is treated confidentially. I do not sell personal data and I do not share proposal or application material with third parties, unless this is explicitly agreed with the client or required by law.

You may request deletion of your material by email.
